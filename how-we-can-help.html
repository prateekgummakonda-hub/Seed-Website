How We Can Help

1. Support seed banks

Seed banks protect and store thousands of traditional seeds for the future.

2. Encourage local farming

Buying from local farmers helps them maintain diverse crops.

3. School projects & awareness

Students can organise exhibitions, create websites and raise awareness.

4. Grow your own plants

Even small home gardens contribute to protecting seed diversity.
