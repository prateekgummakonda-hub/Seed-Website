Protecting the Invisible Library of Seeds

Every seed carries unique genes, colours and flavours. When we lose seed diversity, we lose future options for food security, climate resilience and culture.

About this website

This website was created as a school project to explain what seed diversity is, why it is important, and realistic actions students and schools can take to protect it.
