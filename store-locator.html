This interactive map shows a prototype list of seed and agriculture stores in the Hyderabad region. In a full version, the locator could be expanded to other cities and states.

How this feature could grow in the future

More regions

The same approach could be used to map seed stores, seed banks and community seed libraries across India or globally, helping farmers and students find diverse planting material.

More data layers

Additional information could include which stores carry local varieties, organic seeds, indigenous crops or climate-resilient options, as well as opening times and contact details.
