My Seed Diversity Project

About my project

This project focuses on raising awareness about seed diversity through a website, research, visuals and interactive tools.

What I created

  • A complete educational website
  • A seed store locator prototype
  • Interactive tools (filtering, comparison, maps)
  • Research pages explaining threats & actions

Reflection

Through this project I learned how important seeds are for the environment, farming and climate change. I also learned real website development skills.
