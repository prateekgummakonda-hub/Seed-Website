Threats to Seed Diversity

Loss of traditional farming

Farmers are moving towards fewer hybrid varieties, causing old local seeds to disappear.

Climate change

Extreme temperature and rainfall patterns destroy crops and their genetic variety.

Industrial agriculture

Large-scale farming prefers uniform crops. This reduces natural genetic variation.

Pollution & soil degradation

Chemicals and poor soil management reduce seed health and plant survival.
